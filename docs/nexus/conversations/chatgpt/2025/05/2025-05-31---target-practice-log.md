>

> 
> It‚Äôs primarily used when:
> - The target appears **suddenly**
> - You're reacting to **unexpected movement**
> - You need to **switch quickly** between targets
> 
> ---
> 
> ### ‚öôÔ∏è Characteristics
> 
> - **Speed**: The aim is fast and reactive.
> - **Muscle Memory**: Success relies heavily on memorizing distances between common crosshair positions and where enemies typically appear.
> - **Precision**: You don‚Äôt correct or adjust ‚Äî you land the shot *instantly* or miss.
