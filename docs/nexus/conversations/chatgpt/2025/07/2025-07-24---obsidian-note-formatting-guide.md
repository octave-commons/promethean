> 
> The agent experiences a combined field:
> $$
> \vec{F}_{\text{total}}(\vec{x}, t) = \sum_i w_i(t) F_i(\vec{x}, t)

> ## Field Construction

> $$
> F_i(\vec{x}, t) = -\nabla \phi_i(\vec{x}, t)
