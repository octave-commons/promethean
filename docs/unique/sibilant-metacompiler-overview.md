### üî• Key Feature

* It‚Äôs not bound by the semantics of its host ‚Äî only by the shape of the target

## üß™ Example: Define Async Functions in Sibilant

Let‚Äôs say Sibilant didn‚Äôt support `async` functions.

You could write:


