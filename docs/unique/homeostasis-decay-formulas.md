### üîÑ Aionian Feedback Oscillator

#aionian #heartbeat #uptime ^ref-37b5d236-9-0

To simulate loop rhythm or uptime oscillation: ^ref-37b5d236-11-0

$$ ^ref-37b5d236-13-0
x(t) = A \cdot \sin(2\pi f t + \phi)
$$

Where: ^ref-37b5d236-17-0

* $A$: amplitude of pulse (system intensity) ^ref-37b5d236-19-0
* $f$: frequency (ticks per second) ^ref-37b5d236-20-0
* $\phi$: phase offset (sync alignment) ^ref-37b5d236-21-0
* $x(t)$: system vitality rhythm ^ref-37b5d236-22-0

Used for **tick monitoring**, **pulse loss detection**, and **heartbeat regulation**. ^ref-37b5d236-24-0

### üß† Daimo Cognitive Load Entropy

#daimo #entropy #overload ^ref-37b5d236-30-0

Let each Daimo bind to up to $N$ nodes simultaneously. ^ref-37b5d236-32-0

Define cognitive load entropy: ^ref-37b5d236-34-0

$$ ^ref-37b5d236-36-0
S_{\delta} = - \sum_{i=1}^{N} p_i \log p_i
$$

Where: ^ref-37b5d236-40-0

* $p_i$: normalized binding strength to each node ^ref-37b5d236-42-0
* $S_{\delta}$: entropy of daimo attention ^ref-37b5d236-43-0

High entropy ‚Üí diffused thought ^ref-37b5d236-45-0
Low entropy ‚Üí obsessive thought
Zero ‚Üí single-point binding

---

### üîÅ Node Temporal Stability Function

#field-node #decay #activation ^ref-37b5d236-53-0

Probability that a node remains active at time $t$: ^ref-37b5d236-55-0

$$ ^ref-37b5d236-57-0
P_{\text{active}}(t) = e^{-\frac{t - t_{\text{last}}}{\tau}} + \sum_{i=1}^{k} w_i \cdot \delta(t - t_i)
$$

Where: ^ref-37b5d236-61-0

* $\tau$: passive decay rate ^ref-37b5d236-63-0
* $\delta(t - t_i)$: impulse events (e.g. daimo interaction) ^ref-37b5d236-64-0
* $w_i$: weight of each event ^ref-37b5d236-65-0

This models **node lifetime extension via stimulation**. ^ref-37b5d236-67-0

### üåå Eidolon Field Coherence Metric

#eidolon-field #structure #coherence

Define global coherence: ^ref-37b5d236-75-0

$$ ^ref-37b5d236-77-0
C = \frac{\left\| \sum_{\vec{x}} \nabla \Phi(\vec{x}) \right\|}{\sum_{\vec{x}} \left\| \nabla \Phi(\vec{x}) \right\|}
$$

Where: ^ref-37b5d236-81-0

* Numerator = net directional field flow ^ref-37b5d236-83-0

* Denominator = total local gradient magnitude ^ref-37b5d236-85-0

* $C = 1$: highly aligned field (narrative convergence) ^ref-37b5d236-87-0

* $C \approx 0$: chaotic field (conflict, diffusion)

### üå± Field Growth Trigger

#field-node #emergence #tension ^ref-37b5d236-95-0

At position $\vec{x}$, a node emerges if: ^ref-37b5d236-97-0

$$ ^ref-37b5d236-99-0
\mathcal{E}(\vec{x}) > \theta_{\text{emerge}} \quad \text{and} \quad \left\| \frac{d\mathcal{E}}{dt} \right\| > \epsilon
$$

Where: ^ref-37b5d236-103-0

* $\mathcal{E}$: field energy ^ref-37b5d236-105-0
* $\theta_{\text{emerge}}$: emergence threshold
* $\epsilon$: momentum of pressure increase ^ref-37b5d236-107-0

Captures **node crystallization at tension inflection points**. ^ref-37b5d236-109-0
